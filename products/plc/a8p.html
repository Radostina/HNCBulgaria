HC-A8P Series PLC

HC-A8P, is the original dual system bus, and now become much more faster. The extensible compact PLC is designed to seamlessly control communication network and analog positioning system. With maximum of 256 controllable Input / Output the HC-A8 uses its power and flexibility to provide solutions for variety of applications. A8P can matching A8C right extension modules directly, no conversion needed any more.
